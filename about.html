About Sher-e-Punjab

Discover our rich heritage and commitment to exceptional hospitality

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the visionary hotelier Sardar Jasbir Singh, Sher-e-Punjab Hotel & Restaurant began as a modest establishment with a grand dream - to showcase the rich culture and hospitality of Punjab in the heart of Rajasthan.

Over the decades, we have grown from a small family-run business to one of Abu Road's most prestigious hotels, while never losing sight of our core values: authentic hospitality, cultural pride, and unwavering commitment to guest satisfaction.

Today, under the leadership of the second generation, we continue to blend traditional Punjabi warmth with modern luxury, creating unforgettable experiences for guests from around the world.

Our Vision

To be the premier destination hotel in Rajasthan, recognized globally for our authentic cultural experiences, exceptional service, and commitment to preserving and sharing the rich heritage of Punjab and Rajasthan.

Our Mission

To provide our guests with unparalleled hospitality that reflects the warmth and generosity of Punjabi culture, while offering modern amenities and services that exceed expectations in comfort, dining, and cultural immersion.

Our Core Values

Authentic Hospitality

We treat every guest as family, embodying the traditional Punjabi spirit of "Atithi Devo Bhava" - the guest is God.

Excellence

We strive for perfection in every detail, from our accommodations to our cuisine, ensuring exceptional experiences.

Sustainability

We are committed to preserving our environment and supporting local communities through responsible tourism practices.

Our Journey Through Time

1985 - The Beginning

Sardar Jasbir Singh establishes Sher-e-Punjab Hotel with just 12 rooms, focusing on authentic Punjabi hospitality.

1992 - First Expansion

Added 20 more rooms and launched "The Royal Spice" restaurant, introducing authentic Punjabi cuisine to Rajasthan.

2001 - Modern Amenities

Introduced air conditioning, modern bathrooms, and conference facilities to meet evolving guest expectations.

2010 - Luxury Upgrade

Complete renovation and addition of premium suites, spa services, and enhanced dining options.

2018 - Digital Transformation

Launched online booking platform and implemented smart room technologies while maintaining traditional charm.

2025 - Sustainable Future

Continuing our legacy with eco-friendly initiatives and expanded cultural programs for an authentic Rajasthani experience.
